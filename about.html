Our Story

Established in 2025, Kerala Tigers was created to represent the heart of Kerala – fearless, proud and always ready to fight for every inch. Starting as a group of passionate players and coaches, the club quickly grew into a structured program with a clear vision: develop champions on and off the field.


Every training session is designed around discipline, sportsmanship and tactical intelligence. We believe that the true strength of a team comes from unity, preparation and the support of fans who roar with us in every moment.
